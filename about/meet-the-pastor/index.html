Meet the Pastor

Shaniqua Karanja

Shaniqua Karanja, Pastor of Adullam Mission House, is a compassionate pastoral counselor and ordained minister who brings warmth and expertise to her work.

With a degree in pastoral counseling and a Master of Divinity, along with advanced certifications in marriage therapy and temperament analysis, Shaniqua specializes in helping individuals and families navigate grief, anxiety, relationships, and emotional and religious healing.

Her caring approach fosters a safe space for growth and restoration.
