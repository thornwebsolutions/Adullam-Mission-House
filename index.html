About Us

Adullam Mission House was founded on the belief that every broken person deserves a chance to be restored and launched into a purposeful life.

Just as David found refuge in the cave of Adullam, our mission is to provide a place of refuge, healing, and community. We are dedicated to helping you discover the purpose the Lord has revealed through your pain and equip you to use that experience to help others.

Located in the heart of Blythewood, South Carolina, we welcome all who seek healing, hope, and a supportive community on their journey toward restoration.
